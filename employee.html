Retirement

At A's Bar and Grill, we believe that what makes a good business is how people earn a living. Now, that doesn't mean we believe it fully, but the idea is there.
At 4 SVC per 1.02 hours, that equates 628 of your 'earth years' of work to earn 401K in retirement funds.
Anually, you can earn about 2 SVC in tips. At A's Bar and Grill, we will not take any of that in tax.
As for the 401K needed for retirement, a 0.7% tax will deduct about $2,870.
We do have alternate plans available for those employees that want to earn more towards their retirement.
You can choose to allocate more of your paycheck or less to the fund dynamically.
